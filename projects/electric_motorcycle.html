Project Overview

As Controls Systems Lead and Sensor Integration Lead for GHOST Electric Motorcycles, I spearheaded the development of advanced control systems for a high-performance electric motorcycle platform. This project involved designing and implementing sophisticated feedback control algorithms, electrical systems architecture, and real-time data acquisition systems to optimize performance and enable data-driven tuning.
