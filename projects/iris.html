About the Robot:

  1. Mechanical: Mechanical designs and fabricates the entire robot. We work closely with the other subteams to validate space claims, as well as motor placement.
  2. Electrical: The electrical team is responsible for the development of custom control boards and the electrical connections of the robot.
  3. Autonomy: Autonomy develops the algorithms for SLAM using ROS. Theya re responsible for developing object detection and control of the drive system.
