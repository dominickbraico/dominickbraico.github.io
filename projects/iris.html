Illinois Robotics in Space (IRIS)

Project: NASA Lunabotics

This project showcases the design and implementation of an advanced industrial robotic arm. The robotic arm is equipped with state-of-the-art sensors and actuators, making it highly versatile and precise in various manufacturing tasks.
